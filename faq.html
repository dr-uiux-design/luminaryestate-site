1. Can I buy property with cryptocurrency?

Yes, at Luminary Estate we specialise in facilitating real estate transactions using cryptocurrency. We can help you convert your digital currency into fiat currency, enabling you to buy property in various crypto-friendly jurisdictions.

2. How does the process work?

Our process is straightforward. It involves a free initial consultation, property selection, cryptocurrency to fiat conversion, managing paperwork and due diligence, finalizing the purchase, and providing after-sales support.

3. What cryptocurrencies do you accept?

We accept a wide range of popular cryptocurrencies including Bitcoin, Ethereum and others. Please feel free to contact us for specific enquiries.

4. What kind of properties can I buy with my cryptocurrency?

We offer a wide range of property options, from residential to commercial, in various crypto-friendly countries.

5. How do you deal with the volatility of cryptocurrencies?

To mitigate the potential risks associated with cryptocurrency volatility, we convert your cryptocurrency into fiat currency before completing the property transaction.

6. Can I buy property anywhere with my cryptocurrency?

We specialise in facilitating property purchases in crypto-friendly jurisdictions such as Thailand, Indonesia, UAE, Spain, Portugal.

7. Do you offer post purchase support?

Yes, we offer comprehensive post purchase support including property management services to ensure you continue to enjoy the benefits of your investment.

8. What’s the fee for Luminary Estate’s cryptocurrency property service?

At Luminary Estate our fee is a flat 10% of the property price. This covers our turnkey service, from converting crypto to fiat to handling the paperwork. Additional costs, which are usually small, may vary depending on the specifics of each transaction.

9. Is my transaction secure?

Absolutely. At Luminary Estate we take the security of your transactions very seriously. We maintain a high level of transparency, providing you with all the necessary details every step of the way.

10. How do I start the process?

To get started, contact us for a free initial consultation. Our experienced team will guide you through the process and answer any further questions you may have.
