Luminary Estate is a premium property service. The company provides support for purchasing and selling real estate for cryptocurrency in countries loyal to this payment method.

This is a service for crypto investors who want to invest their cryptocurrency in real estate. Depending on the chosen country, Luminary Estate, alongside local partners, will help you remotely select the object, watch it with the realtor, prepare everything you need to deal with concerning lawyers, and accompany the transfer to the seller and signing of the property contract.

The purchase can take place both remotely and live—all for your comfort.
